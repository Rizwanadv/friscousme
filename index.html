Our American Story

Made in the USA.
Made for the World.

Frisco was founded in 2016 in Dania Beach, Florida with one goal — to deliver premium quality at honest prices. Within years, it became the most recognized pet essentials brand in the country. Now, we're ready for the world.

2016
Founded · Dania Beach, Florida, USA
Frisco launches as an independent pet essentials brand. First products: collars, leashes, and beds.
2020
National Recognition
Frisco reaches 300+ SKUs and becomes the top-selling pet essentials brand in America — beating major global names.
2023
500+ Products, 10M+ Pets
Cat trees, airline carriers, crates, harnesses — Frisco now covers every stage of pet life across America.
NOW
Middle East Expansion · 2025
For the first time, Frisco crosses the Atlantic — bringing the full American Frisco experience to Gulf pet owners.
